FAQs

Why should I choose WordPress for my website?

WordPress is a versatile and powerful platform that offers a wide range of features and functionalities. It is user-friendly, highly customizable, and has a large community of developers and users who contribute to its continuous improvement. Whether you need a simple blog or a complex e-commerce site, WordPress can meet your needs.

Do you use templates for WordPress websites?

No, at Onedesigns Studio, I create fully customized websites without using any templates. Each website is designed and developed from scratch based on your unique requirements and Figma designs, ensuring a truly personalized and unique online presence.

How long does it take to develop a custom WordPress website?

The timeline for developing a custom WordPress website depends on the complexity and scope of the project. On average, it takes 4-8 weeks to complete a website from initial consultation to launch. I will provide you with a detailed timeline after discussing your specific needs.

What kind of support do you offer after the website is launched?

I offer comprehensive maintenance and support services to ensure your website remains up-to-date, secure, and fully functional. This includes regular updates, performance optimization, security checks, and troubleshooting any issues that may arise.

Can you help with SEO for my WordPress website?

Yes, I provide SEO services to help improve your website’s visibility on search engines. My SEO strategies include keyword research, on-page optimization, content creation, and link building to help drive organic traffic to your site.
